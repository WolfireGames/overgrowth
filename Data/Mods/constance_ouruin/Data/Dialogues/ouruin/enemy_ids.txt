Enemy IDs and names for each level in Scar of Ouruin.
Used for lethality & enemy state scripts, namely this variable:
    ouruin_[levelname]_enemy_[id]_status
		0: awake
		1: unconscious
		2: dead




:=: POINTS CODEX

	eliminations
		Add 1 lethality point
	kills
		Add 8 lethality points
	zonekills
		Add 2 lethality points. Incurred upon drowning, dying to spikes, or lava
	overkills
		Add 2 lethality points per overkill. Maximum of 10 overkills per level for a total of 20 points
	throatscut
		Add 4 lethality points
    


    At the end of every level, Ouruin adds up each elimination, kill and overkill. It also collects the names of each character.
	These variables are then tallied to form a total number of points.
	This point total is compared to a percentage of the maximum possible lethality points (not counting throats slit and overkills).

	Perfect Non-Lethal Stealth requires 0% lethality points.
	Non-Lethal Stealth requires less than 10% lethality points.
	Non-Lethal requires less than 25% lethality points.
	Lethal requires less than 50% lethality points.
	Full-Lethal requires more than 75% lethality points.
	Perfect Full-Lethal requires 100% lethality points.
	Senseless Full-Lethal requires more than 100% lethality points.
